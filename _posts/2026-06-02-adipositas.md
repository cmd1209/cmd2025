The Adipositas Info website was developed to provide patients, families and healthcare professionals with a clear and accessible source of information about obesity and its treatment options. The project focused on transforming complex medical content into a structured digital experience that helps users better understand available therapies, treatment pathways and long-term care.

{% include post-gallery.html %}

Built within an established WordPress environment, the website combines intuitive navigation, responsive design and carefully organized content to improve accessibility across devices. Special attention was given to creating a trustworthy and supportive user experience that encourages exploration while reducing barriers to understanding medical information.

The result is a modern healthcare platform that makes complex topics easier to navigate and supports informed decision-making through clarity, structure and thoughtful design.
