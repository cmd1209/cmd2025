Creative Director / Front-End Designer & Developer / Photographer

In February 1999, I landed my first professional job at an advertising agency in Essen, Germany. I crafted my first Bewerbung using Microsoft Word and convinced my parents to invest $100 to send my B.F.A. portfolio—packed in my now-infamous silver case. I was hired on the spot and asked if I could start the following Monday.

That Monday in early February 1999 set into motion one of the most amazing, terrifying, complicated, and eye-opening work experiences I could have imagined. Over the years, my journey has evolved beyond traditional design, embracing front-end development and the endless possibilities of digital interaction. Alongside my passion for crafting thoughtful user experiences, photography has remained a constant in my creative life—another way to capture, frame, and tell stories.

Art Direction

Great design tells a story. As an art director, I shape visual narratives that connect, inspire, and leave a lasting impression. Whether crafting brand identities, designing campaigns, or refining aesthetics, I thrive on bringing creative visions to life with clarity and purpose.

Frontend Design & Development

Bridging the gap between design and technology, I create intuitive, engaging digital experiences. From pixel-perfect UI design to seamless front-end interactions, I bring ideas to life with clean, efficient code—always keeping usability and aesthetics in perfect balance.

Photography

Photography is my way of capturing moments that words can’t express. From conceptual shoots to documentary-style storytelling, I focus on composition, light, and emotion to create images that resonate. Every frame is an opportunity to distill an experience into something timeless.
